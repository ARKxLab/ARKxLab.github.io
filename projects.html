Projects

Complete Projects

Twilight Rohingya

We designed a 360  VR videos-based system to depict real-life scenarios from a refugee camp with active and passive navigation.

Learn more

Virtual Human for ASD

The study aims to use virtual humans to teach children with autism to express themselves through a conversational manner.

Learn more

StarRescue

StarRescue is a two-player collaborative tablet game that aims to enhance autistic children’s social skills. It augments children’s attention paying to others, perspective-taking, and negotiation by visualizing the turn-taking collaboration process.

Learn more

Privacy for Families with Autism

This study explores the privacy issues among families with autism by interviewing parents of children with autism. It collects perceptions and concerns of families with autism in their daily lives and information exchange online, and the protection behaviors they have taken.

Learn more

Measuring Anxiety

The goal of this study is to use machine learning algorithms to measure, predict and design interventions for stress and anxiety. While the ability to detect, access, and intervene with such disorders is crucial for the well-being of the general population, traditional self-assessments for mental health have certain limitations due to their subject nature.

Learn more

Entangled Habitation

We explore the entangled habitation design concept to advocate a more-than-human perspective in human-nature interaction design. Following the entangled habitation design concept, we implemented three plant-based wearables to reframe human interactions with plants.

Learn more

I Never Imagined Grandma Could Do So Well with Technology

Drawing on a qualitative study based on semi-structured interviews with 20 older adults and 18 younger ones in China, we unpack this long-term process, as well as the evolving roles of family members

Learn more

Ongoing Projects

Virtual Pet

This project aims to design and generate virtual pets (VPs) based on their personality and appearance traits. To understand how VPs' appearance traits relate to their personality, we followed design guidelines from the Five Factor Model (FFM).

Learn more

Virtual Diagnostic

To design more comprehensive and feasible strategies for assessing fear of intimacy, we turned to a Virtual Reality (VR) paradigm using role-play scenarios to evaluate the in-game behavior of subjects, including decision-making, distance-to-a-character, and biometric data.

Learn more

VTuber Project

To uncover the nuances in the creator's perceptions towards the creation of VTuber's avatars and to understand potential challenges in the creating process. we developed an assisting tool/system for the creator community to improve their working experiences of designing VTuber's avatars.

Learn more

AR Geo-location at DKU

AR Geo-location at DKU is a location-based augmented reality App designed for DKU Community. Community members scan the buildings around DKU campus to check out relevant information or build up social interactions with others through this online platform.

Learn more

Role-playing Murder Mystery

This study aims to generate a unified definition of Jubensha, as well as investigate its impact on social interactions and players’ emotion through various mechanisms within Jubensha.

Learn more
