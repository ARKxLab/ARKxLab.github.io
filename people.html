Lab orgnizer

Prof. Xin Tong

Xin’s research contributes to the larger understanding of how people with physical and psychological disabilities experience and interact with technology. Her work largely centers on understanding, designing, developing, and evaluating interactive systems, such as games and VR environments.

Research Assistant

Yihe Wang

I am a HCI researcher currently working as a research assistant at Duke Kunshan University. My research interests are in human-computer interaction with a focus on designing and developing interactive technologies for children and families.
Research Assistant

Rongqi Bei

Research Assistant

Hongni Ye
